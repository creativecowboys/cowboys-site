Q. How long does SEO take to work for a Franklin business?

Q. What does SEO cost for a Williamson County small business?
A. Our Local Foundations tier is $1,500/mo and is right for most single-location Franklin small businesses with under $1M revenue. Anyone quoting under $1,000/mo is doing template work that won't compete with the Seaboard cluster.
Q. What's the difference between local SEO and regular SEO?
A. Local SEO is the work that makes you show up in the 'map pack' (the 3 businesses Google shows above regular results when someone searches a local query). It's GBP optimization, local citations, reviews, and location-specific landing pages. Regular SEO is the work that makes you rank for non-local queries. Most Franklin small businesses need 80% local SEO and 20% regular SEO. Most Williamson County eCom businesses need the opposite.
Q. Do I need both SEO and Google Ads?
A. Often yes, especially early. Google Ads buys you visibility while SEO is still compounding (months 1–6). Then ads can dial down as organic takes over — our average Franklin client cuts ad spend 40–60% by month 9. Some businesses need to keep both running indefinitely (highly competitive markets, time-sensitive promotions). We can tell you which after a free audit.
Q. Will I rank in Cool Springs / Westhaven / Downtown Franklin specifically?
A. If we do the work right, yes. We build neighborhood-specific landing pages with neighborhood-specific schema and target neighborhood-specific keywords. 'HVAC Cool Springs' and 'HVAC Westhaven' are different searches with different competitors and different ranking factors. We treat them that way.
Q. What's AI Overview optimization and do I need it?
A. AI Overviews are the AI-generated summaries Google shows at the top of search results for many queries. Getting cited as a source in those summaries means your business is recommended by the AI. The optimization is mostly: clean FAQ schema, clear entity disambiguation (Wikidata, Wikipedia, structured data), authoritative content on the questions buyers ask. We've earned 34 AI Overview citations across active clients. Do you need it? If your buyers research before they buy, yes.

We're a senior team doing the 2026 work: AI Overview optimization, schema-heavy local SEO, real link building from real publications, content from people who actually know your industry.
